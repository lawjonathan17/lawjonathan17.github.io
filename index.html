PROJECTS


Pomodoro Plus

Pomodoro Plus is a countdown timer Android application representing the Pomodoro time management method involving a 25-minute work interval followed by a 5-minute break. I wanted to create this application for my own personal use. The Pomodoro method helped me tremendously in increasing my productivity. It helps me break long study sessions into smaller, reachable parts.

WebSoc+

WebSoc+ is a Google Chrome Extension that adds a link to RateMyProfessor.com next to the instructor's name to bring you to their page on RMP. However, due to violating the Terms of Use of RateMyProfessor, I cannot release this extension to the public.
